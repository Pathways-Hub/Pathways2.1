Cloud 9

by diplodocus

Download

Cloud 9 is a lightweight lofi audio plugin for Pathways Workspaces

Built to integrate seamlessly into your workflow, Cloud 9 launches quickly, stays out of your way, and provides a consistent, low-effort environment for concentration. The clean design and effortless functionality make it ideal for anyone looking to bring more rhythm and calm into their workspace without breaking momentum.

From soft textures and mellow beats to warm audio layers that fade into the background, Cloud 9 acts as an ambient companion — not just a music player, but a mindset enhancer. Created by diplodocus, it’s more than sound — it’s part of your creative process.
